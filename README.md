# Zen Garden

A <cl>Geode Mod</c> that adds the <cp>Zen Garden</c> from <cg>Plants Vs. Zombies</c> into <cl>Geometry Dash!</c>

## Features
- <cb>Feed</c> and <cg>Grow your Players</c>
- <cp>Customize your Garden</c> with <co>Various Player Icons</c>
- Use <cl>Diamond Shards</c> to purchase new players <cr>*(This uses your actual Diamond Shards)*</c>

## Tutorial
- Go to the <co>"Shop"</c> to buy a player <cb>*(first player is free)*</c>
- Go back to your <cg>Zen Garden</c> to see your players <cb>*(texture and color is random)*</c>
- Each player will require different <cl>Orbs</c>, <cy>Stars</c>, <cb>Moons</c>, and <cl>Diamonds</c> to grow
- When you feed your player, there is an <co>80% chance</c> you get <cl>10 Diamond Shards</c>, <co>17% chance</c> you get <cl>100 Diamond Shards</c>, and <co>3% chance</c> you get <cl>500 Diamond Shards</c>.
- You can click on a player to see it's <cf>maturity level</c>, <cg>rename it</c>, or <cr>sell it</c>.
- Once the player is at <cf>maturity level 5</c>, it will <cr>not level up</c> and will <cb>constantly require moons</c>.
- You can move a player by clicking the <co>move button at the top</c>, selecting the player that you want to move, and then the slot you want to move it to.
- Grow your garden!
